Sisig Recipe

Name Quantity
Pork belly, boiled and chopped into small pieces 1 lb
Pig's ears, boiled and chopped into small pieces 1/2 lb
Chicken liver, chopped into small pieces 1/2 lb
large onion, chopped 1
Cloves garlic, minced 4
Green chili peppers, chopped 2-3 pieces
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
Vinegar 2 tbsp
Calamansi juice (or lemon juice) 1 tbsp
Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
Salt and pepper to taste
Cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat a small amount of oil and saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the boiled pork belly, pig's ears, and chicken liver to the pan. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Add the green chili peppers and continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, and calamansi juice to the pan. Mix well and continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the mayonnaise to the pan and stir well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
  8. Enjoy your homemade Sisig!
