Burger Recipe

Name Quantity
Ground beef 1 lb
Humberger buns 4
Cheese 4 slice
Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
ketchup 1/4 cup
Mustard 1 tbsp
Soy Sauce 1 tbsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Salt and Pepper to taste
Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal parts and shape each into a patty.
  3. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill or cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, until cooked through.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty and let it melt.
  6. Mix together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, soy sauce, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  7. Toast the buns on the grill or in a pan.
  8. Assemble the burgers by spreading the sauce on the bottom bun, adding a patty with cheese on top, and then topping with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  9. Ready to Serve
