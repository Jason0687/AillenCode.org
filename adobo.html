Pork Adobo Recipe

Name Quantity
Pork belly, cut into 2-inch cubes 2 lbs
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
White Vinegar 1/2 cup
Soy Sauce 1 cup
Water 1/2 cup
Sugar 1 tbsp
Cloves garlic, minced 6
whole black peppercorns 1 tsp
Bay leaves 2
Cooking oil 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the pork belly and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the garlic and peppercorns and cook for another minute, or until fragrant.
  3. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, water, sugar, and bay leaves to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  4. Remove the lid and continue to simmer for another 10 to 15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened to your liking.
  5. Serve the pork adobo hot over rice, with the sauce spooned over the top.
  6. Enjoy your delicious pork adobo!
